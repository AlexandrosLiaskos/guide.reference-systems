# IEEE Style

## Inline Citation Format

**Uses numbered references in square brackets.**

| Scenario | Inline Format | Example |
|----------|---------------|---------|
| Single source | [#] | [1] |
| Multiple sources | [#], [#] or [#]-[#] | [1], [3] or [1]-[3] |
| Specific page | [#, p. #] | [1, p. 45] |
| Multiple pages | [#, pp. #-#] | [1, pp. 45-50] |

**Rules:**
- Numbers assigned in order of first appearance in text
- Same source always uses same number
- References listed numerically, not alphabetically

---

## Reference List Format & Examples

**Title:** "References" (centered)
**Order:** Numerical (order of first citation)
**Spacing:** Single-spaced, hanging indent

---

### Books

**Format - Single Author:**
```
[#] A. A. Author, Title of Book: Subtitle. City, State/Country: Publisher, Year.
```
**Example:**
```
[1] J. D. Smith, The Complete Guide to Research: Methods and Practice. New York, NY, USA: Academic Press, 2024.
```

---

**Format - Two Authors:**
```
[#] A. A. Author and B. B. Author, Title of Book. City: Publisher, Year.
```
**Example:**
```
[2] M. R. Johnson and K. L. Williams, Research Methods Explained. London, UK: Sage Publications, 2023.
```

---

**Format - Three+ Authors:**
```
[#] A. A. Author, B. B. Author, and C. C. Author, Title of Book. City: Publisher, Year.
```
**Example:**
```
[3] A. Adams, B. Brown, and C. Chen, Collaborative Research Methods. Oxford, UK: Oxford University Press, 2024.
```

---

**Format - Six+ Authors:**
```
[#] A. A. Author et al., Title of Book. City: Publisher, Year.
```
**Example:**
```
[4] J. Smith et al., Advanced Research Techniques. Cambridge, MA, USA: MIT Press, 2024.
```

---

**Format - Edited Book:**
```
[#] A. A. Editor, Ed., Title of Book. City: Publisher, Year.
```
**Example:**
```
[5] R. Martinez, Ed., Handbook of Research Methods. New York, NY, USA: Routledge, 2024.
```

---

**Format - Chapter in Edited Book:**
```
[#] A. A. Author, "Title of chapter," in Title of Book, A. A. Editor, Ed. City: Publisher, Year, pp. #-#.
```
**Example:**
```
[6] S. Thompson, "Data analysis techniques," in Handbook of Research Methods, R. Martinez, Ed. New York, NY, USA: Routledge, 2024, pp. 123-156.
```

---

**Format - Edition Other Than First:**
```
[#] A. A. Author, Title of Book, #th ed. City: Publisher, Year.
```
**Example:**
```
[7] J. D. Smith, Research Basics, 4th ed. New York, NY, USA: Academic Press, 2024.
```

---

### Journal Articles

**Format - Print Journal:**
```
[#] A. A. Author, "Title of article," Journal Name, vol. #, no. #, pp. #-#, Month Year.
```
**Example:**
```
[8] K. L. Williams, "Effects of methodology on outcomes," Journal of Research, vol. 45, no. 3, pp. 234-251, Mar. 2024.
```

---

**Format - Online with DOI:**
```
[#] A. A. Author, "Title of article," Journal Name, vol. #, no. #, pp. #-#, Month Year, doi: DOI.
```
**Example:**
```
[9] M. A. Brown, "Digital research methods," Research Quarterly, vol. 12, no. 2, pp. 45-67, Feb. 2023, doi: 10.1000/example.
```

---

**Format - Early Access/In Press:**
```
[#] A. A. Author, "Title of article," Journal Name, early access, Month Day, Year, doi: DOI.
```
**Example:**
```
[10] R. S. Chen, "Innovative approaches," Science Journal, early access, Mar. 15, 2024, doi: 10.1000/example.
```

---

### Conference Papers

**Format - Published Proceedings:**
```
[#] A. A. Author, "Title of paper," in Proc. Conference Name, City, State/Country, Year, pp. #-#.
```
**Example:**
```
[11] K. Johnson, "New approaches to data analysis," in Proc. IEEE Annual Conf. Research Methods, Chicago, IL, USA, 2024, pp. 45-56.
```

---

**Format - With DOI:**
```
[#] A. A. Author, "Title of paper," in Proc. Conference Name, City, Country, Year, pp. #-#, doi: DOI.
```
**Example:**
```
[12] M. Smith, "Machine learning applications," in Proc. Int. Conf. Artificial Intelligence, London, UK, 2024, pp. 123-130, doi: 10.1000/example.
```

---

**Format - Unpublished/Presented:**
```
[#] A. A. Author, "Title of paper," presented at the Conference Name, City, Country, Month Day, Year.
```
**Example:**
```
[13] L. Davis, "Emerging technologies," presented at the IEEE Tech Symp., San Francisco, CA, USA, May 15, 2024.
```

---

### Websites

**Format - With Author:**
```
[#] A. A. Author. "Title of page." Website Name. URL (accessed Month Day, Year).
```
**Example:**
```
[14] T. Johnson. "Understanding research methods." Research Hub. https://www.researchhub.org/methods (accessed Mar. 20, 2024).
```

---

**Format - Organization Author:**
```
[#] Organization Name. "Title of page." URL (accessed Month Day, Year).
```
**Example:**
```
[15] IEEE. "Publication guidelines." https://www.ieee.org/guidelines (accessed Mar. 20, 2024).
```

---

**Format - No Author:**
```
[#] "Title of page." Website Name. URL (accessed Month Day, Year).
```
**Example:**
```
[16] "Research strategies." Academic Resource. https://www.example.com/strategies (accessed Mar. 20, 2024).
```

---

### Technical Reports

**Format - Numbered Report:**
```
[#] A. A. Author, "Title of report," Organization, City, State/Country, Rep. #, Month Year.
```
**Example:**
```
[17] J. Smith and M. Jones, "Analysis of outcomes," Research Institute, Boston, MA, USA, Tech. Rep. TR-2024-15, Mar. 2024.
```

---

**Format - Government Report:**
```
[#] Organization, "Title of report," Dept., City, State/Country, Rep. #, Year.
```
**Example:**
```
[18] National Institute of Standards and Technology, "Cybersecurity framework," U.S. Dept. Commerce, Washington, DC, USA, Rep. 2024-001, 2024.
```

---

### Standards

**Format:**
```
[#] Title of Standard, Standard #, Organization, Year.
```
**Example:**
```
[19] IEEE Standard for Software Quality Assurance Processes, IEEE Std 730-2014, IEEE, 2014.
```

---

### Patents

**Format:**
```
[#] A. A. Inventor, "Title of patent," Country Patent # Kind, Month Day, Year.
```
**Example:**
```
[20] J. Smith, "Method for data processing," U.S. Patent 10 123 456 B2, Nov. 15, 2024.
```

---

### Dissertations & Theses

**Format - PhD Dissertation:**
```
[#] A. A. Author, "Title of dissertation," Ph.D. dissertation, Dept., Univ., City, State/Country, Year.
```
**Example:**
```
[21] R. T. Williams, "Research methodology in practice," Ph.D. dissertation, Dept. Comput. Sci., Univ. Michigan, Ann Arbor, MI, USA, 2024.
```

---

**Format - Master's Thesis:**
```
[#] A. A. Author, "Title of thesis," M.S. thesis, Dept., Univ., City, Country, Year.
```
**Example:**
```
[22] L. M. Chen, "Innovative research approaches," M.S. thesis, Dept. Elect. Eng., Stanford Univ., Stanford, CA, USA, 2024.
```

---

### Manuals & Datasheets

**Format - Manual:**
```
[#] Title of Manual, Organization, City, State/Country, Year.
```
**Example:**
```
[23] Arduino Programming Manual, Arduino, Turin, Italy, 2024.
```

---

**Format - Datasheet:**
```
[#] "Component name datasheet," Company, City, Country, Datasheet #, Year. [Online]. Available: URL
```
**Example:**
```
[24] "ATmega328P datasheet," Microchip Technology, Chandler, AZ, USA, DS40002061B, 2024. [Online]. Available: https://www.microchip.com/xxxxx
```

---

### Software & Code

**Format - Software:**
```
[#] A. A. Author. Title of Software (Version #). Organization. [Online]. Available: URL
```
**Example:**
```
[25] Microsoft Corporation. Microsoft Excel (Version 16.0). Microsoft. [Online]. Available: https://www.microsoft.com/excel
```

---

**Format - GitHub Repository:**
```
[#] A. A. Author. "Repository name." GitHub. URL (accessed Month Day, Year).
```
**Example:**
```
[26] J. Smith. "Data-analysis-toolkit." GitHub. https://github.com/jsmith/data-analysis-toolkit (accessed Mar. 20, 2024).
```

---

### Audiovisual Media

**Format - Video:**
```
[#] A. A. Author. Title of Video. (Month Day, Year). Accessed: Month Day, Year. [Online Video]. Available: URL
```
**Example:**
```
[27] TED. The Future of Research. (Mar. 10, 2024). Accessed: Mar. 20, 2024. [Online Video]. Available: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xxxxx
```

---

**Format - Podcast:**
```
[#] A. A. Host. "Title of episode." Podcast Name. (Month Day, Year). Accessed: Month Day, Year. [Podcast]. Available: URL
```
**Example:**
```
[28] J. Smith. "Research methods explained." The Research Podcast. (Mar. 5, 2024). Accessed: Mar. 20, 2024. [Podcast]. Available: https://www.spotify.com/xxxxx
```

---

### Social Media

**Format - Tweet/Post:**
```
[#] A. A. Author. "Content up to 20 words." Platform. Month Day, Year. [Online]. Available: URL
```
**Example:**
```
[29] B. Gates. "Excited to share new research findings on climate change solutions." Twitter. Mar. 10, 2024. [Online]. Available: https://twitter.com/BillGates/status/xxxxx
```

---

### Data Sets

**Format:**
```
[#] A. A. Author, "Title of data set," Version #, Publisher, Year, doi: DOI.
```
**Example:**
```
[30] J. Smith, "National survey data 2024," Version 1.0, ICPSR, 2024, doi: 10.1000/example.
```

---

### Legal Sources

**Format - Law/Act:**
```
[#] Title of Act, Public Law #, # Stat. #, Year.
```
**Example:**
```
[31] Americans with Disabilities Act, Public Law 101-336, 104 Stat. 327, 1990.
```
