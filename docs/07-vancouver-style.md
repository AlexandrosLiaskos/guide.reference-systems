# Vancouver Style

## Inline Citation Format

**Uses numbered references in parentheses or superscript.**

| Scenario | Format | Example |
|----------|--------|---------|
| Single source | (#) or หฃ | (1) or ยน |
| Multiple sequential | (#-#) | (1-3) |
| Multiple non-sequential | (#,#) | (1,3,5) |
| With page number | (#, p#) | (1, p45) |

**Rules:**
- Numbers assigned in order of first appearance
- Same source always uses same number
- References listed numerically, not alphabetically
- Superscript format common in medical journals

---

## Reference List Format & Examples

**Title:** "References" (centered)
**Order:** Numerical (order of first citation)
**Spacing:** Single-spaced

**Author format:** Up to 6 authors list all; 7+ list first 6 then "et al."

---

### Books

**Format - Single Author:**
```
#. Author AA. Title of book: subtitle. Edition. Place: Publisher; Year.
```
**Example:**
```
1. Smith JD. The complete guide to research: methods and practice. New York: Academic Press; 2024.
```

---

**Format - Two to Six Authors:**
```
#. Author AA, Author BB, Author CC. Title of book. Place: Publisher; Year.
```
**Example:**
```
2. Johnson MR, Williams KL. Research methods explained. London: Sage Publications; 2023.
```

---

**Format - Seven+ Authors:**
```
#. Author AA, Author BB, Author CC, Author DD, Author EE, Author FF, et al. Title. Place: Publisher; Year.
```
**Example:**
```
3. Adams A, Brown B, Chen C, Davis D, Evans E, Foster F, et al. Collaborative research methods. Oxford: Oxford University Press; 2024.
```

---

**Format - Edited Book:**
```
#. Editor AA, editor. Title of book. Place: Publisher; Year.
```
**Example:**
```
4. Martinez R, editor. Handbook of research methods. New York: Routledge; 2024.
```

---

**Format - Chapter in Edited Book:**
```
#. Author AA. Title of chapter. In: Editor BB, editor. Title of book. Place: Publisher; Year. p. #-#.
```
**Example:**
```
5. Thompson S. Data analysis techniques. In: Martinez R, editor. Handbook of research methods. New York: Routledge; 2024. p. 123-56.
```

---

**Format - Edition Other Than First:**
```
#. Author AA. Title of book. #th ed. Place: Publisher; Year.
```
**Example:**
```
6. Smith JD. Research basics. 4th ed. New York: Academic Press; 2024.
```

### Journal Articles

**Format - Standard Article:**
```
#. Author AA, Author BB. Title of article. Journal Name. Year;Volume(Issue):Pages.
```
**Example:**
```
7. Williams KL, Brown MA. Effects of methodology on outcomes. J Clin Res. 2024;45(3):234-51.
```

---

**Format - With DOI:**
```
#. Author AA. Title of article. Journal Name. Year;Volume(Issue):Pages. doi: DOI
```
**Example:**
```
8. Brown MA. Digital research methods. Res Q. 2023;12(2):45-67. doi: 10.1000/example
```

---

**Format - Online Ahead of Print:**
```
#. Author AA. Title of article. Journal Name. Year [Epub ahead of print]. doi: DOI
```
**Example:**
```
9. Chen RS. Innovative approaches. Sci J. 2024 [Epub ahead of print]. doi: 10.1000/example
```

---

**Format - Journal Article Online:**
```
#. Author AA. Title of article. Journal Name [Internet]. Year [cited Year Mon Day];Volume(Issue):Pages. Available from: URL
```
**Example:**
```
10. Davis LM. Treatment protocols. J Med [Internet]. 2024 [cited 2024 Mar 20];15(2):100-15. Available from: https://www.example.com/article
```

---

### Websites

**Format - With Author:**
```
#. Author AA. Title of page [Internet]. Place: Publisher; Year [updated Year Mon Day; cited Year Mon Day]. Available from: URL
```
**Example:**
```
11. Johnson T. Understanding research methods [Internet]. Boston: Research Hub; 2024 [updated 2024 Mar 15; cited 2024 Mar 20]. Available from: https://www.researchhub.org/methods
```

---

**Format - Organization Author:**
```
#. Organization. Title of page [Internet]. Place: Publisher; Year [cited Year Mon Day]. Available from: URL
```
**Example:**
```
12. World Health Organization. Research guidelines [Internet]. Geneva: WHO; 2024 [cited 2024 Mar 20]. Available from: https://www.who.int/guidelines
```

---

**Format - No Author:**
```
#. Title of page [Internet]. Place: Publisher; Year [cited Year Mon Day]. Available from: URL
```
**Example:**
```
13. Clinical research strategies [Internet]. New York: Medical Resource; 2024 [cited 2024 Mar 20]. Available from: https://www.example.com/strategies
```

---

### Newspapers & Magazines

**Format - Newspaper (Print):**
```
#. Author AA. Title of article. Newspaper Name. Year Mon Day;Sect. A:page.
```
**Example:**
```
14. Martinez S. New research findings released. New York Times. 2024 Mar 20;Sect. A:1.
```

---

**Format - Newspaper (Online):**
```
#. Author AA. Title of article. Newspaper Name [Internet]. Year Mon Day [cited Year Mon Day]. Available from: URL
```
**Example:**
```
15. Johnson L. Study reveals new insights. Washington Post [Internet]. 2024 Mar 20 [cited 2024 Mar 21]. Available from: https://www.washingtonpost.com/example
```

---

### Reports

**Format - Government/Organization Report:**
```
#. Organization. Title of report. Place: Publisher; Year. Report No.: #.
```
**Example:**
```
16. National Institutes of Health. Clinical research standards. Bethesda (MD): NIH; 2024. Report No.: 2024-001.
```

---

**Format - Technical Report:**
```
#. Author AA. Title of report. Place: Organization; Year. Report No.: #.
```
**Example:**
```
17. Smith J, Jones M. Analysis of treatment outcomes. Boston: Research Institute; 2024. Report No.: TR-2024-15.
```

---

### Conference Papers

**Format - Published Proceedings:**
```
#. Author AA. Title of paper. In: Editor BB, editor. Title of proceedings; Year Mon Day-Day; Place. City: Publisher; Year. p. #-#.
```
**Example:**
```
18. Johnson K. New approaches to clinical trials. In: Smith J, editor. Proceedings of the Annual Medical Convention; 2024 May 15-18; Chicago. New York: Medical Press; 2024. p. 45-56.
```

---

**Format - Paper from Conference:**
```
#. Author AA. Title of paper. Paper presented at: Conference Name; Year Mon Day-Day; City, Country.
```
**Example:**
```
19. Williams R. Methodology in clinical research. Paper presented at: International Health Conference; 2024 Jun 10-12; London, UK.
```

---

### Dissertations & Theses

**Format:**
```
#. Author AA. Title of dissertation [dissertation]. Place: University; Year.
```
**Example:**
```
20. Williams RT. Research methodology in clinical practice [dissertation]. Ann Arbor: University of Michigan; 2024.
```

### Software & Apps

**Format:**
```
#. Title of software [computer program]. Version #. Place: Publisher; Year.
```

### Data Sets

**Format:**
```
#. Author AA. Title of data set [data set]. Version #. Place: Distributor; Year. doi: DOI
```
**Example:**
```

```

### Legal/Legislative Sources

**Format - Law:**
```
#. Title of law, Statute source (Year).
```
**Example:**
```
28. Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act of 1996, Pub. L. No. 104-191 (1996).
```

### Social Media

**Format - Tweet/Post:**
```
#. Author AA. Content up to first 20 words [Internet]. Platform; Year Mon Day [cited Year Mon Day]. Available from: URL
```
**Example:**
```
29. Gates B. Excited to share new research findings on global health initiatives [Internet]. Twitter; 2024 Mar 10 [cited 2024 Mar 20]. Available from: https://twitter.com/BillGates/status/xxxxx
```

## Journal Abbreviations

Vancouver style uses standard journal title abbreviations. Examples:

| Full Title | Abbreviation |
|-----------|--------------|
| New England Journal of Medicine | N Engl J Med |
| Journal of the American Medical Association | JAMA |
| British Medical Journal | BMJ |
| The Lancet | Lancet |
| Annals of Internal Medicine | Ann Intern Med |
| Journal of Clinical Investigation | J Clin Invest |
