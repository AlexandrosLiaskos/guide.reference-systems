# APA Style (7th Edition)

## Inline Citation Format

**Basic format:** `(Author, Year)` or `Author (Year)`

| Scenario | Inline Format | Example |
|----------|---------------|---------|
| One author | (Author, Year) | (Smith, 2024) |
| Two authors | (Author & Author, Year) | (Smith & Jones, 2024) |
| Three+ authors | (First Author et al., Year) | (Smith et al., 2024) |
| Organization | (Organization, Year) | (World Health Organization, 2024) |
| No author | (Title, Year) | (*Study Results*, 2024) |
| No date | (Author, n.d.) | (Smith, n.d.) |
| Direct quote | (Author, Year, p. #) | (Smith, 2024, p. 45) |
| Multiple sources | (Author, Year; Author, Year) | (Adams, 2023; Brown, 2024) |

---

## Bibliography Format & Examples

**Title:** "References" (centered, bold)
**Order:** Alphabetical by author surname
**Spacing:** Double-spaced, hanging indent

---

### Books

**Format - Single Author:**
```
Author, A. A. (Year). Title of book: Subtitle. Publisher. DOI or URL
```
**Example:**
```
Smith, J. D. (2024). The complete guide to research: Methods and practice. Academic Press. https://doi.org/10.1000/example
```

---

**Format - Two Authors:**
```
Author, A. A., & Author, B. B. (Year). Title of book. Publisher.
```
**Example:**
```
Johnson, M. R., & Williams, K. L. (2023). Research methods explained. Sage Publications.
```

---

**Format - Three to Twenty Authors:**
```
Author, A. A., Author, B. B., Author, C. C., & Author, D. D. (Year). Title. Publisher.
```
**Example:**
```
Adams, A., Brown, B., Chen, C., Davis, D., & Evans, E. (2024). Collaborative research. Oxford University Press.
```

---

**Format - Twenty-One+ Authors:**
```
Author, A., Author, B., ... Author, S., . . . Author, Z. (Year). Title. Publisher.
```
*List first 19 authors, then ellipsis (...), then final author*

---

**Format - Edited Book:**
```
Editor, E. E. (Ed.). (Year). Title of book. Publisher.
```
**Example:**
```
Martinez, R. (Ed.). (2024). Handbook of research methods. Routledge.
```

---

**Format - Chapter in Edited Book:**
```
Author, A. A. (Year). Title of chapter. In E. E. Editor (Ed.), Title of book (pp. #-#). Publisher.
```
**Example:**
```
Thompson, S. (2024). Data analysis techniques. In R. Martinez (Ed.), Handbook of research methods (pp. 123-156). Routledge.
```

---

**Format - Edition Other Than First:**
```
Author, A. A. (Year). Title of book (Xth ed.). Publisher.
```
**Example:**
```
Smith, J. D. (2024). Research basics (4th ed.). Academic Press.
```

---

### Journal Articles

**Format - With DOI:**
```
Author, A. A. (Year). Title of article. Journal Name, Volume(Issue), Page-Page. DOI
```
**Example:**
```
Williams, K. L. (2024). Effects of methodology on outcomes. Journal of Research, 45(3), 234-251. https://doi.org/10.1000/example
```

---

**Format - Without DOI (Print):**
```
Author, A. A. (Year). Title of article. Journal Name, Volume(Issue), Page-Page.
```
**Example:**
```
Brown, M. A. (2023). Traditional research approaches. Research Quarterly, 12(2), 45-67.
```

---

**Format - Online Without DOI:**
```
Author, A. A. (Year). Title of article. Journal Name, Volume(Issue), Page-Page. URL
```
**Example:**
```
Chen, R. S. (2024). Digital research methods. E-Journal of Science, 8(1), 12-28. https://www.examplejournal.com/article123
```

---

### Websites

**Format - Webpage with Author:**
```
Author, A. A. (Year, Month Day). Title of page. Site Name. URL
```
**Example:**
```
Johnson, T. (2024, March 15). Understanding research methods. Research Hub. https://www.researchhub.org/methods
```

---

**Format - Webpage with Organization Author:**
```
Organization Name. (Year). Title of page. URL
```
**Example:**
```
American Psychological Association. (2024). Publication guidelines. https://www.apa.org/pubs/guidelines
```

---

**Format - Webpage No Date:**
```
Author, A. A. (n.d.). Title of page. Site Name. URL
```
**Example:**
```
Smith, R. (n.d.). Research strategies. Academic Resource. https://www.example.com/strategies
```

---

### Newspapers & Magazines

**Format - Print Newspaper:**
```
Author, A. A. (Year, Month Day). Title of article. Newspaper Name, pp. Page-Page.
```
**Example:**
```
Martinez, S. (2024, March 20). New research findings released. The New York Times, pp. A1, A4.
```

---

**Format - Online Newspaper:**
```
Author, A. A. (Year, Month Day). Title of article. Newspaper Name. URL
```
**Example:**
```
Johnson, L. (2024, March 20). Study reveals new insights. The Washington Post. https://www.washingtonpost.com/example
```

---

**Format - Magazine:**
```
Author, A. A. (Year, Month). Title of article. Magazine Name, Volume(Issue), Page-Page.
```
**Example:**
```
Brown, K. (2024, April). The science of discovery. Scientific American, 330(4), 42-49.
```

---

### Reports

**Format - Government Report:**
```
Organization. (Year). Title of report (Publication No. XXX). Publisher. URL
```
**Example:**
```
National Institute of Health. (2024). Annual research report (Publication No. 2024-001). U.S. Government Printing Office. https://www.nih.gov/report
```

---

**Format - Technical Report:**
```
Author, A. A. (Year). Title of report (Report No. XXX). Organization.
```
**Example:**
```
Smith, J., & Jones, M. (2024). Analysis of outcomes (Report No. TR-2024-15). Research Institute.
```

---

### Dissertations & Theses

**Format - Published (Database):**
```
Author, A. A. (Year). Title of dissertation [Doctoral dissertation, University Name]. Database Name.
```
**Example:**
```
Williams, R. T. (2024). Research methodology in practice [Doctoral dissertation, University of Michigan]. ProQuest Dissertations and Theses Global.
```

---

**Format - Unpublished:**
```
Author, A. A. (Year). Title of dissertation [Unpublished doctoral dissertation]. University Name.
```
**Example:**
```
Chen, L. M. (2024). Innovative research approaches [Unpublished doctoral dissertation]. Stanford University.
```

---

### Conference Papers

**Format - Paper Presentation:**
```
Author, A. A. (Year, Month Day-Day). Title of paper [Paper presentation]. Conference Name, Location.
```
**Example:**
```
Johnson, K. (2024, May 15-18). New approaches to data analysis [Paper presentation]. Annual Research Convention, Chicago, IL, United States.
```

---

**Format - Poster:**
```
Author, A. A. (Year, Month Day-Day). Title of poster [Poster presentation]. Conference Name, Location.
```
**Example:**
```
Smith, M. L. (2024, May 15-18). Methodology comparison study [Poster presentation]. Annual Research Convention, Chicago, IL, United States.
```

---

### Audiovisual Media

**Format - Film:**
```
Director, D. D. (Director). (Year). Title of film [Film]. Production Company.
```
**Example:**
```
Nolan, C. (Director). (2010). Inception [Film]. Warner Bros.
```

---

**Format - YouTube Video:**
```
Author/Channel. (Year, Month Day). Title of video [Video]. YouTube. URL
```
**Example:**
```
TED. (2024, March 10). The future of research [Video]. YouTube. https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xxxxx
```

---

**Format - Podcast Episode:**
```
Host, H. H. (Host). (Year, Month Day). Title of episode (No. XX) [Audio podcast episode]. In Podcast Name. Publisher. URL
```
**Example:**
```
Smith, J. (Host). (2024, March 5). Research methods explained (No. 45) [Audio podcast episode]. In The Research Podcast. Spotify. https://www.spotify.com/xxxxx
```

---

**Format - TV Series Episode:**
```
Writer, W. W. (Writer), & Director, D. D. (Director). (Year, Month Day). Title of episode (Season X, Episode Y) [TV series episode]. In Producer, P. P. (Executive Producer), Series name. Production Company.
```
**Example:**
```
Smith, A. (Writer), & Jones, B. (Director). (2024, January 15). The discovery (Season 2, Episode 5) [TV series episode]. In Williams, C. (Executive Producer), Science explorers. Netflix.
```

### Social Media

**Format - Tweet/X Post:**
```
Author, A. A. [@username]. (Year, Month Day). Content up to 20 words [Tweet]. Twitter. URL
```
**Example:**
```
Gates, B. [@BillGates]. (2024, March 10). Excited to share new research findings on climate change solutions [Tweet]. Twitter. https://twitter.com/BillGates/status/xxxxx
```

---

**Format - Facebook Post:**
```
Author, A. A. (Year, Month Day). Content up to 20 words [Status update]. Facebook. URL
```
**Example:**
```
NASA. (2024, March 15). New images from the Mars rover reveal surprising geological features [Status update]. Facebook. https://www.facebook.com/NASA/posts/xxxxx
```

---

**Format - Instagram Post:**
```
Author, A. A. [@username]. (Year, Month Day). Content up to 20 words [Photograph]. Instagram. URL
```
**Example:**
```
National Geographic [@natgeo]. (2024, March 12). Stunning view of the aurora borealis captured in Iceland [Photograph]. Instagram. https://www.instagram.com/p/xxxxx
```

### Legal Sources

**Format - Court Case:**
```
Name v. Name, Volume Source Page (Court Year). URL
```
**Example:**
```
Brown v. Board of Education, 347 U.S. 483 (1954). https://www.law.cornell.edu/supremecourt/text/347/483
```

---

**Format - Statute/Law:**
```
Name of Act, Title Source ยง Section (Year). URL
```
**Example:**
```
Americans With Disabilities Act of 1990, 42 U.S.C. ยง 12101 et seq. (1990). https://www.ada.gov
```

---

### Software & Apps

**Format - Software:**
```
Author, A. A. (Year). Title of software (Version X.X) [Computer software]. Publisher. URL
```
**Example:**
```
Microsoft Corporation. (2024). Microsoft Excel (Version 16.0) [Computer software]. https://www.microsoft.com/excel
```

---

**Format - Mobile App:**
```
Developer. (Year). Title of app (Version X.X) [Mobile app]. App Store or Google Play. URL
```
**Example:**
```
Headspace Inc. (2024). Headspace (Version 4.0) [Mobile app]. App Store. https://apps.apple.com/app/headspace
```

---

### Data Sets

**Format:**
```
Author, A. A. (Year). Title of data set (Version X) [Data set]. Publisher. DOI or URL
```
**Example:**
```
Smith, J., & Research Team. (2024). National survey data 2024 (Version 1.0) [Data set]. ICPSR. https://doi.org/10.1000/example
```
