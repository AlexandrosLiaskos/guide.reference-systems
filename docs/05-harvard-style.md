# Harvard Style

## Inline Citation Format

**Basic format:** `(Author Year)` or `(Author Year, p. #)`

| Scenario | Inline Format | Example |
|----------|---------------|---------|
| One author | (Author Year) | (Smith 2024) |
| One author with page | (Author Year, p. #) | (Smith 2024, p. 45) |
| Two authors | (Author & Author Year) | (Smith & Jones 2024) |
| Three+ authors | (Author et al. Year) | (Smith et al. 2024) |
| Organization | (Organization Year) | (World Health Organization 2024) |
| No author | (Title Year) | (*Research Report* 2024) |
| No date | (Author n.d.) | (Smith n.d.) |
| Direct quote | (Author Year, p. #) | (Smith 2024, p. 45) |
| Multiple sources | (Author Year; Author Year) | (Adams 2023; Brown 2024) |
| Multiple works same author/year | (Author Year a, Year b) | (Smith 2024a, 2024b) |

---

## Bibliography Format & Examples

**Title:** "Reference List" (centered)
**Order:** Alphabetical by author surname
**Spacing:** Double-spaced, hanging indent

---

### Books

**Format - Single Author:**
```
Author, A.A. (Year) Title of book: subtitle. Place: Publisher.
```
**Example:**
```
Smith, J.D. (2024) The complete guide to research: methods and practice. New York: Academic Press.
```

---

**Format - Two Authors:**
```
Author, A.A. & Author, B.B. (Year) Title of book. Place: Publisher.
```
**Example:**
```
Johnson, M.R. & Williams, K.L. (2023) Research methods explained. London: Sage Publications.
```

---

**Format - Three+ Authors:**
```
Author, A.A., Author, B.B. & Author, C.C. (Year) Title of book. Place: Publisher.
```
**Example:**
```
Adams, A., Brown, B. & Chen, C. (2024) Collaborative research methods. Oxford: Oxford University Press.
```

---

**Format - Edited Book:**
```
Editor, E.E. (ed.) (Year) Title of book. Place: Publisher.
```
**Example:**
```
Martinez, R. (ed.) (2024) Handbook of research methods. New York: Routledge.
```

---

**Format - Chapter in Edited Book:**
```
Author, A.A. (Year) 'Title of chapter', in Editor, E.E. (ed.) Title of book. Place: Publisher, pp. #-#.
```
**Example:**
```
Thompson, S. (2024) 'Data analysis techniques', in Martinez, R. (ed.) Handbook of research methods. New York: Routledge, pp. 123-156.
```

---

**Format - Edition Other Than First:**
```
Author, A.A. (Year) Title of book. #th edn. Place: Publisher.
```
**Example:**
```
Smith, J.D. (2024) Research basics. 4th edn. New York: Academic Press.
```

---

**Format - E-book:**
```
Author, A.A. (Year) Title of book. Place: Publisher. Available at: URL (Accessed: Day Month Year).
```
**Example:**
```
Smith, J.D. (2024) Research methods today. New York: Academic Press. Available at: https://www.example.com/book (Accessed: 20 March 2024).
```

---

### Journal Articles

**Format - Print Journal:**
```
Author, A.A. (Year) 'Title of article', Journal Name, Volume(Issue), pp. #-#.
```
**Example:**
```
Williams, K.L. (2024) 'Effects of methodology on outcomes', Journal of Research, 45(3), pp. 234-251.
```

---

**Format - Online with DOI:**
```
Author, A.A. (Year) 'Title of article', Journal Name, Volume(Issue), pp. #-#. doi: DOI.
```
**Example:**
```
Brown, M.A. (2023) 'Digital research methods', Research Quarterly, 12(2), pp. 45-67. doi: 10.1000/example.
```

---

**Format - Online without DOI:**
```
Author, A.A. (Year) 'Title of article', Journal Name, Volume(Issue), pp. #-#. Available at: URL (Accessed: Day Month Year).
```
**Example:**
```
Chen, R.S. (2024) 'Innovative approaches', E-Journal of Science, 8(1), pp. 12-28. Available at: https://www.example.com/article (Accessed: 15 March 2024).
```

---

### Websites

**Format - With Author:**
```
Author, A.A. (Year) Title of page. Available at: URL (Accessed: Day Month Year).
```
**Example:**
```
Johnson, T. (2024) Understanding research methods. Available at: https://www.researchhub.org/methods (Accessed: 20 March 2024).
```

---

**Format - Organization Author:**
```
Organization (Year) Title of page. Available at: URL (Accessed: Day Month Year).
```
**Example:**
```
World Health Organization (2024) Research guidelines. Available at: https://www.who.int/guidelines (Accessed: 20 March 2024).
```

---

**Format - No Author:**
```
Title of page (Year) Available at: URL (Accessed: Day Month Year).
```
**Example:**
```
Research strategies for students (2024) Available at: https://www.example.com/strategies (Accessed: 20 March 2024).
```

---

**Format - No Date:**
```
Author, A.A. (no date) Title of page. Available at: URL (Accessed: Day Month Year).
```
**Example:**
```
Smith, R. (no date) Data collection methods. Available at: https://www.example.com/data (Accessed: 20 March 2024).
```

---

### Newspapers & Magazines

**Format - Print Newspaper:**
```
Author, A.A. (Year) 'Title of article', Newspaper Name, Day Month, p. #.
```
**Example:**
```
Martinez, S. (2024) 'New research findings released', The Times, 20 March, p. 5.
```

---

**Format - Online Newspaper:**
```
Author, A.A. (Year) 'Title of article', Newspaper Name, Day Month. Available at: URL (Accessed: Day Month Year).
```
**Example:**
```
Johnson, L. (2024) 'Study reveals new insights', The Guardian, 20 March. Available at: https://www.theguardian.com/example (Accessed: 21 March 2024).
```

---

**Format - Magazine:**
```
Author, A.A. (Year) 'Title of article', Magazine Name, Month, pp. #-#.
```
**Example:**
```
Brown, K. (2024) 'The science of discovery', New Scientist, April, pp. 42-49.
```

---

### Reports

**Format - Government Report:**
```
Organization (Year) Title of report. Place: Publisher. Available at: URL (Accessed: Day Month Year).
```
**Example:**
```
Department of Health (2024) Annual health report 2024. London: HMSO. Available at: https://www.gov.uk/report (Accessed: 20 March 2024).
```

---

**Format - Technical Report:**
```
Author, A.A. (Year) Title of report. Report No. Organization.
```
**Example:**
```
Smith, J. & Jones, M. (2024) Analysis of outcomes. Report No. TR-2024-15. Research Institute.
```

---

### Dissertations & Theses

**Format - Published (Database):**
```
Author, A.A. (Year) Title of dissertation. PhD thesis. University. Available at: URL (Accessed: Day Month Year).
```
**Example:**
```
Williams, R.T. (2024) Research methodology in practice. PhD thesis. University of Manchester. Available at: https://www.proquest.com/xxxxx (Accessed: 20 March 2024).
```

---

**Format - Unpublished:**
```
Author, A.A. (Year) Title of dissertation. Unpublished PhD thesis. University.
```
**Example:**
```
Chen, L.M. (2024) Innovative research approaches. Unpublished PhD thesis. University of Oxford.
```

---

### Conference Papers

**Format - Published Proceedings:**
```
Author, A.A. (Year) 'Title of paper', in Editor, E.E. (ed.) Title of proceedings. Place: Publisher, pp. #-#.
```
**Example:**
```
Johnson, K. (2024) 'New approaches to data analysis', in Smith, J. (ed.) Proceedings of the Annual Research Convention. London: Academic Press, pp. 45-56.
```

---

**Format - Unpublished:**
```
Author, A.A. (Year) 'Title of paper'. Paper presented at Conference Name, Location, Day-Day Month.
```
**Example:**
```
Smith, M.L. (2024) 'Methodology comparison study'. Paper presented at Annual Research Convention, Chicago, 15-18 May.
```

---

### Audiovisual Media

**Format - Film:**
```
Title (Year) Directed by First Last [Film]. Place: Production Company.
```
**Example:**
```
Inception (2010) Directed by Christopher Nolan [Film]. Burbank: Warner Bros.
```

---

**Format - YouTube Video:**
```
Author/Channel (Year) Title of video. Available at: URL (Accessed: Day Month Year).
```
**Example:**
```
TED (2024) The future of research. Available at: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xxxxx (Accessed: 20 March 2024).
```

---

**Format - Podcast:**
```
Author, A.A. (Year) 'Title of episode', Podcast Name [Podcast]. Day Month. Available at: URL (Accessed: Day Month Year).
```
**Example:**
```
Smith, J. (2024) 'Research methods explained', The Research Podcast [Podcast]. 5 March. Available at: https://www.spotify.com/xxxxx (Accessed: 20 March 2024).
```

---

**Format - TV Programme:**
```
Title of programme (Year) Channel, Day Month.
```
**Example:**
```
Horizon: The science of discovery (2024) BBC Two, 15 March.
```

---

### Social Media

**Format - Tweet/X:**
```
Author, A.A. or @username (Year) Tweet content. [Twitter] Day Month. Available at: URL (Accessed: Day Month Year).
```
**Example:**
```
@BillGates (2024) Excited to share new research findings on climate change solutions. [Twitter] 10 March. Available at: https://twitter.com/BillGates/status/xxxxx (Accessed: 20 March 2024).
```

---

**Format - Facebook:**
```
Author/Page (Year) Post content. [Facebook] Day Month. Available at: URL (Accessed: Day Month Year).
```
**Example:**
```
NASA (2024) New images from the Mars rover reveal surprising geological features. [Facebook] 15 March. Available at: https://www.facebook.com/NASA/posts/xxxxx (Accessed: 20 March 2024).
```

---

### Legal Sources

**Format - Act/Statute:**
```
Title of Act Year (Jurisdiction).
```
**Example:**
```
Data Protection Act 2018 (UK).
```

---

**Format - Case:**
```
Case name [Year] Report abbreviation Volume Page.
```
**Example:**
```
Donoghue v Stevenson [1932] AC 562.
```

---

### Software & Apps

**Format:**
```
Developer (Year) Title of software (Version #) [Computer program]. Available at: URL (Accessed: Day Month Year).
```
**Example:**
```
Microsoft Corporation (2024) Microsoft Excel (Version 16.0) [Computer program]. Available at: https://www.microsoft.com/excel (Accessed: 20 March 2024).
```

---

### Data Sets

**Format:**
```
Author, A.A. (Year) Title of data set (Version #) [Data set]. Publisher. doi: DOI or Available at: URL.
```
**Example:**
```
Smith, J. (2024) National survey data 2024 (Version 1.0) [Data set]. UK Data Service. doi: 10.1000/example.
```
